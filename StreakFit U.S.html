StreakFit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2025

StreakFit ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our StreakFit iOS application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the App’s functionality:

a. Information You Provide

  • Fitness Data: When you log workouts, set goals, or track streaks, we collect data like exercise type, duration, and notes you input.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address or Apple ID (via Sign in with Apple) for authentication.
  • User Content: Optional profile details, such as your name or preferences, that you choose to provide.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • HealthKit Data: With your permission, we access Apple HealthKit data (e.g., heart rate, steps, calories burned) to sync with your workouts.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized data on App usage, such as feature interactions, session duration, and crash reports, to improve performance.
  • Device Information: We collect device details like iOS version, device model, and unique identifiers (e.g., IDFA, with consent) for analytics and troubleshooting.

c. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect sensitive personal information like precise location, financial data, or social security numbers.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts unless explicitly added as a feature and disclosed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core App features, like tracking workouts, displaying streaks, and syncing with Apple Watch/HealthKit.
  • Personalize your experience, such as offering goal recommendations based on your fitness data.
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance App performance and fix bugs.
  • Send notifications or reminders (with your consent) to help you stay on track.
  • Comply with legal obligations or respond to legal requests.
